# Rewind

A puzzle-platformer where the player can **rewind time** and interact with their past selves to solve challenges and complete each level.

## How to Play

1. Move and interact to progress through the level
2. When stuck, **rewind time**
3. Your previous actions play automatically, becoming a helper
4. Work alongside multiple past selves to reach the goal
